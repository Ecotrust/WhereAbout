Map Tool Overview

Please take a moment to read over the following information before you begin the mapping portion of this survey. This survey is to map your {{interview.shape_name_plural}} along the Oregon Coast only. You will create a map for only one {{interview.resource_name}} at a time for each of your user groups (i.e. a map for salmon via kayak and a separate map for salmon via private vessel).
To begin you will be presented with a map of the Oregon Coast.
  • Use the map to focus in on your {{interview.shape_name_plural}}
  • You will be able to 'move around' or 'pan' across the map
  • You can 'zoom' in to your {{interview.shape_name_plural}}
Once you zoom into your area you will then use the tool to draw your {{interview.shape_name_plural}} on the map.
  • Draw only the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} you deem important
  • Draw {{interview.shape_name_plural}} from your cumulative years of experience
Afterwards you will assign value to each {{interview.shape_name}} in the form of 'pennies'.
  • Distribute 100 pennies across your {{interview.shape_name_plural}}
  • Assigning pennies to each {{interview.shape_name}} you draw
  • Allocate more pennies to the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} that are more important to you
  • EXAMPLE: The {{interview.shape_name}} with 50 pennies is more important than either of the other two
  • Note that the pennies of the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} add up to 100

If you require any additional help or clarifications, please refer to our frequently asked questions page. Also, please do not hesitate to email fish@ecotrust.org if you need more help.
