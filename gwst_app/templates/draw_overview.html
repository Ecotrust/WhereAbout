Map Tool Overview

Please take a moment to read over the following information before you begin the mapping portion of this survey. The purpose of this survey is to map your {{interview.shape_name_plural}} along the {{interview.region.name}} only. You will create a map for one {{interview.resource_name}} at a time.
First, you will be presented with a map of {{interview.region.name}}.
  • You will be able to 'move around' the map
  • You will 'zoom' in to your {{interview.shape_name_plural}}
Next, you will draw your {{interview.shape_name_plural}} on the map.
  • For each {{interview.resource_name}}, draw the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} you went to last year
Finally, you will assign value to each {{interview.shape_name}} in the form of 'pennies'.
  • Distribute 100 pennies across your {{interview.shape_name_plural}}
  • Allocate more pennies to the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} that are more important to you
  • (See example to the left): The {{interview.shape_name}} with 50 pennies is valued as more important than the other two. Note that the pennies of the {{interview.shape_name_plural}} add up to 100

If you require any additional help or clarifications, please do not hesitate to email fish@ecotrust.org.
