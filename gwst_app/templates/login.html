Oregon is currently amending its Territorial Sea Plan by developing policies and procedures to help guide the location of wave energy generators. One of these policies (Goal 19) states that we must "conserve marine resources and ecological functions for the purpose of providing long-term ecological, economic, and social value and benefits to future generations".

To do this, Ecotrust has been contracted by the state of Oregon to conduct a comprehensive mapping project that will assess the socioeconomic importance of ocean areas to the coastal community. This information will then be used to help inform the location of wave energy facilities so that impacts to existing human uses may be minimized.

If you'd like more information about our project please visit our Oregon TSP page or the DLCD TSP page.
