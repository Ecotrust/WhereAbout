• Drawing your shapes

    Right click on a resource group in the "My Shapes" window, then select "Add new shape" from its menu to draw a new shape for that resource group.

    In draw mode, left-click to define points for your shape. Double-click to place the last point and finish the new shape.

  • Assigning pennies to your shapes

    Once you have drawn one or more shapes, double-click them (either on the map or in the "My Shapes" window) to open their attribute form.

    Enter a penny value between 1 and 100 for the shape, as well as any notes you wish to send us about more specific boundaries for your shape, then click the "Save" button.

  • Using the map

    You can move the map by clicking-and-dragging it, or by clicking the blue arrows in the upper right.

    Zoom using your mouse wheel, or the blue plus and minus buttons in the upper right, or clicking on the segmented vertical line in the upper right.

  • Displaying nautical charts, depth contours, satellite imagery, lat/lon lines

    Click the checkbox to the left of any map name in the "Maps" window to display it.

    If you display multiple maps and want them ordered differently on the screen, you can click and drag a map name to a different position in the "Maps" window to rearrange it.
